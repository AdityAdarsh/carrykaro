CarryKaro

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

CarryKaro ("we", "us", "our") operates carrykaro.live, a peer-to-peer platform connecting package senders with travellers. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google.
  • Profile information: City, role (sender/carrier/both), travel frequency, and optionally your phone number — all provided by you.
  • Listings and activity: Delivery requests and trips you post, matches you initiate, and messages you send on the platform.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, events, IP address, device type, and browser information — collected via PostHog analytics to help us understand how the product is used.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the platform — matching senders with carriers and enabling communication between them.
  • To send you notifications about matches and messages relevant to your listings.
  • To improve the product using aggregated, anonymised usage analytics.
  • To respond to support requests or feedback you submit.

3. How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth. We do not store your Google password. Analytics data is processed by PostHog on US-based servers.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these cases:

  • Between users: When a match is created, basic profile information (name, city) is visible to the other party so they can coordinate the delivery.
  • Service providers: Supabase (database and auth), PostHog (analytics), Tally.so (feedback forms). These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect the safety of users.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use browser localStorage to store your session and notification read-state. PostHog may set cookies for analytics purposes. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party ad networks.

6. Data Retention

Your account and listing data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us. Anonymised analytics data may be retained longer.

7. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, email us at adityai81011@gmail.com.

8. Children

CarryKaro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. For material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you — for example, by updating the date prominently or notifying you via the platform.

10. Contact
